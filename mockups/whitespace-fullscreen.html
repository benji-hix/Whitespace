Whitespace — Fullscreen Mockup

Three states × two themes. Centered text column, font-height block cursor, vertical fade mask, film grain.

1. Resting state

Just the centered column, vertical fade mask, grain background. No overlays. Block cursor sits in the middle of the visible column (typewriter centering keeps the active line vertically centered).
